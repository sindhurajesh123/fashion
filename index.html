Rohit Bal, the designer

Ask anyone who knows ABC of fashion to spell the word, and he or she will most likely spell it as R-o-h-i-t-B-a-l. Rohit Bal is among the top fashion designers in India and a legend whose flamboyant style transpires designs like no other. Rohit Bal was born in Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir, India. The history student from St. Stephens College shifted gears when he decided to pursue fashion design from the prestigious National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) in Delhi. In 1986, Rohit Bal and his brother Rajiv Bal set up an export company Orchid Oversea Pvt. Ltd. In 1990, Rohit Bal launched an eponymous designer label with his first independent collection that got him noticed in the high fashion corridors. From then to date, he has ruled the fashion industry and spread his designer abilities and business capabilities to other ventures in India and overseas.


Rohit Bal, the designer, combines the right mix of history, folklore, village craft, and dying arts to create imaginative and innovative masterpieces for catwalks and fashion talks. His sense of aesthetics and meticulous attention to detail demonstrates changing landscape in Indian designer wear. Rohit's versatility as a designer has helped him gain appreciation across continents and earned him a place among the Best Indian Fashion Designers.
